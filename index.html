Ecoplasm

Ecoplasm is a 2023 startup company that was conceptualized in October 2022 by two environmental enthusiasts, Gula Bhaskar Reddy and Avinash Narayanswamy. Gula works for Shell Netherlands while Avinash is a greenpreneur. Ecoplasm specializes in three major products namely non forest-based dhoop sticks, dishwash liquids, and herbal floor cleaners. This start-up strongly believes in the principles of sustainable development and incorporates them in the products.
